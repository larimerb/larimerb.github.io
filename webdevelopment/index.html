What if the best storytelling lesson came from a five-year old?

The inspiration for BryX Studio didn't come from a boardroom or a business plan. It came from watching our five-year-old turn a handful of colorful bricks into incredible creations, and then tell stories about them with confidence, imagination, and joy. That's when we realized something important:every meaningful story starts with a collection of pieces.
